1 Covid-19 death in 24hrs, positivity rate 0.85%

Nov 13, 2022 05:02 PM

One person died from Covid-19 in 24 hours till 8 am today (November 13, 2022), said a press release issued by the Directorate General of Health Services (DGHS). The total number of deaths from Covid-19 is currently 29,427 in the country and the death rate stands at 1.45 percent. At least 35 new coronavirus cases were recorded during this period. The daily positivity rate is 0.85 percent while the total positivity rate stands at 13.53 percent. With the new infections, the total number of cases rose to 20,36,166, the release added.

A total of 4,114 samples were tested across the country during this period. At least 183 Covid-19 patients have recovered within this time. The total number of recoveries now stands at 19,83,761 and the recovery rate at 97.43 percent, said the release. The lone deceased was a female, aged between 61-70 years old, added the release.
