Our Mission

As fellow graduate students at East West University(EWU) in 2022, Nahid, Marwa and Rafsan noticed a shift in the way people's need of update summarization. In a modern life consumers are supposed to get information continously to prevent or protect the future Obstruction.

From this shift, this project was born: Update Summarizer. It was founded on "Capstone", the notion that people don't want to be interrupted for not having any tools like this â€” they want to be helped.

Today, the Update Summarizer continues to empower businesses around the world to stop obstacles, start helping, and return their focus to the customer.
